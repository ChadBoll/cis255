The first TEDxSVSU was held Saturday, August 1, 2015 on the SVSU campus at the Rhea Miller Recital Hall.

Speaker Schedule

There were two types of presentations, speakers and exhibits. Speakers presented their talks according to the schedule below. The rules for TEDx events require 25% of all speaker presentations to be prior TED or TEDx videos. There were 10 speakers and 4 videos as shown below.


Exhibits were on display during registration, lunch and coffee breaks. For full list of exhibits, click EXHIBITS tab, above.
